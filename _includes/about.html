30x30 is SFTR's transit improvement initiative to develop a network of rapid lines that truly ties San Francisco together with end-to-end service in 30 minutes by the year 2030.

Drop a pin anywhere in San Francisco and see how far you can reach in 30 minutes using public transit using local bus and light rail routes. Select a rapid route (by clicking its checkbox) and see how much farther you can reach in the same 30 minute transit time.

This visualization tool was originally forked from the open source project Mapnificent, which was created by Stefan Wehrmeyer.
